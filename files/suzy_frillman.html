Suzy Frillman

AB Education, MAT Early Childhood Education

Suzy Frillman has taught in the early education field since 1996. She joined the WUNS staff in 2001. Her undergraduate degree is in art education (K – 12) from Missouri State University. She also attained a Master of Arts in early childhood education from Webster University.

Suzy teaches in the Big Bear classroom and is the art specialist at WUNS, bringing her passion for teaching art through literature and hands-on experiences to the children. She also coordinates the afternoon Bear Tracks program. Suzy believes that WUNS is a special place to teach because of the diversity of the families and the school’s commitment to the developmental needs of the students.

She is married to her terrific husband Adam and her lovely daughter Sydney (a nursery school alum). Outside of school, Suzy enjoys time with her family, taking walks, enjoying nature, making art, practicing yoga, and watching Sydney play soccer.
