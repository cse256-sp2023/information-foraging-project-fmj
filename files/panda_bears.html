Panda Bears

The Panda Bear class is the afternoon session for 2 1/2 – and 3-year-old children. Teachers design a variety of engaging activities that gradually encourage children to interact and communicate with others. The indoor classroom and outdoor playground are safe, interesting spaces for Panda Bears to explore and make sense of their world.

Classroom Goals

The nursery school ensures that our school environment is safe, healthy and conducive to children’s exploration and independence. Our teachers encourage students to:

  • Explore, try and expand their experiences and interests.
  • Express themselves through language, drama, art, music and play.
  • Use their words to solve problems.
  • Recognize the rights, feelings and property of others.
  • Develop their motor coordination.
  • Increase their attention span.
  • Become more independent.
  • Appreciate language, creativity, literacy and numbers in many different forms.
  • Recognize themselves as competent, interested learners.
