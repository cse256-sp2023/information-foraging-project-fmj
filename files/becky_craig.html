Becky Craig

Science Specialist, Naturalist,
Bachelor of Science in Early Childhood Education

Becky is thrilled to continue the Nature on the Move program that has been coming to WUNS for 19 years. Becky holds a Bachelor of Science in Early Childhood Education from the University of Missouri-Columbia. She has taught young children for a total of 18 years, 6 in kindergarten and more recently 12 in Pre-K. Becky and her husband Randy have been married for 26 years and have four children, all currently in college. Becky and Randy love to spend time on their property in Northeastern Missouri near Mark Twain Lake where they enjoy watching and listening to all the wildlife, fishing, boating, and riding their horses. Becky believes that one way to preserve our natural resources is to teach the youngest members of our society to love and respect the natural world around them.
