Bear Cubs

The Bear Cub class is the morning session for 3- and 4-year-old children. Their growing communication and motor skills allow these preschoolers to become more self-sufficient. Teacher guidance and routines in the classroom help build self-esteem and confidence in the children as they enjoy individual, small-group and large-group activities.

Classroom Goals

The nursery school ensures that our school environment is safe, healthy and conducive to children’s exploration and independence. Our teachers encourage students to:

  • Cultivate a child’s positive self image, both as an individual and as a member of the classroom community.
  • Learn how to establish positive, constructive relationships with others as they form friendships and work and play together.
  • Develop independence in a supportive group setting.
  • Care for and respect their learning environment.
  • Communicate openly and honestly with other children, teachers and staff.
  • Enjoy every aspect of their school day and see that learning is fun.
