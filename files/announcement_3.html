Chinese New Year at Lulu’s Seafood: Thursday, January 23, 2020

By ccorbett • November 29, 2019

WUNS families are welcome to celebrate the Chinese New Year with dinner at Lulu’s Seafood in University City on Thursday, January 23. Lulu’s will donate 20% of their sales that night back to the Nursery School. Please mark your calendar and attend a delicious Chinese New Year dinner with WUNS family and friends.
